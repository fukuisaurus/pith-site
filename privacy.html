Pith Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Pith is a personal task manager and daily notebook for iOS and iPadOS. This policy explains how it handles your data.

What Pith stores

Pith stores your tasks, notes, and app settings locally on your device.

Where your data is stored

If you are signed into iCloud on your device, Pith syncs your data to your own private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service. This data is end-to-end controlled by Apple and is not accessible to the developer of Pith. If you are not signed into iCloud, your data stays on your device.

What Pith does NOT do

Pith does not collect analytics, does not send data to any third-party service, does not use advertising SDKs, does not track you across apps or websites, and does not sell or share your data.

Crash reports

During the TestFlight beta period, Apple's TestFlight service may collect crash reports and in-app feedback you submit. This information is handled by Apple under its own privacy policy and is used by the developer only to diagnose bugs.
